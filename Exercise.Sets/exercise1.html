K-POP GROUPS

  1. POPULAR KPOP GROUP

    1. BTS

      • Breaking records as the first K-pop group to perform at the Grammy Awards.
      • Consistently topping the Billboard Hot 100 chart multiple times.
      • Pioneers in global music achievements, setting new standards for K-pop.

    2. BLACKPINK

      • Viral music videos with billions of views on YouTube.
      • High-profile collaborations with artists like Lady Gaga and Dua Lipa.
      • Global fashion icons influencing trends beyond music.

    3. TWICE

      • Consistent chart-toppers with catchy and addictive songs.
      • Massive fanbase in Japan, making them highly influential internationally.
      • Bright and relatable concepts that resonate widely with fans.

  2. K-POP GROUPS WITH RECORD-BREAKING ACHIEVEMENTS

    1. BTS

      • First K-pop group to speak at the United Nations, showcasing their global influence.
      • Multiple Billboard Music Award wins, highlighting their crossover success in Western markets.
      • Continually sets records, becoming a cultural phenomenon beyond music.

    2. BLACKPINK

      • Most-followed girl group on social media, reflecting their vast global fanbase.
      • Holds records for the most-viewed music videos within 24 hours, demonstrating massive digital impact.
      • Known for constantly breaking barriers and redefining success for K-pop girl groups.

    3. TWICE

      • Holds the record for the most music show wins for a K-pop girl group, proving their consistent popularity.
      • Dominates charts in South Korea and Japan, solidifying their status as one of the most successful girl groups.
      • Renowned for their catchy songs and engaging performances, driving their ongoing success.

  3. RISING K-POP GROUPS

    1. IVE

      • Catchy hits and elegant concepts that appeal to a broad audience.
      • Quickly gained popularity due to stunning visuals and undeniable talent.
      • Consistently delivers fresh and trendy music that resonates with fans.

    2. NewJeans

      • Unique approach to pop music with nostalgic sounds and modern twists.
      • Known for their creative and youthful styling that sets them apart.
      • Fresh and innovative image that captures the attention of new fans.

    3. LE SSERAFIM

      • Powerful performances that showcase their fierce stage presence.
      • Bold fashion and visually striking concepts that make a statement.
      • Songs that challenge traditional norms, promoting confidence and empowerment.
